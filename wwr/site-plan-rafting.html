Overview

Purpose

This website aims to promote and provide information about thrilling water rafting adventures for enthusiasts.

Audience

Welcome adventure seekers, nature lovers, and those looking for an adrenaline rush.

Scenario Questions:

  1. Q1: What type of people would be interested in water rafting?
  2. A1: Adventure seekers, nature enthusiasts, and thrill-seekers.
  3. Q2: What age group is the target audience?
  4. A2: Primarily ages 18-45, but all age groups are welcome.
  5. Q3: Are families with children part of the target audience?
  6. A3: Yes, families with older children who meet safety requirements.
